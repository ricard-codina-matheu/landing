The reason behind GreenCaf

In 2020, more than 20 billion capsules were thrown away without being recycled. If we placed them side by side, they would go around the world 14 times!

At GreenCaf, we want to reduce that figure to 0, so we have created a unique solution that allows you to stop spending hundreds of euros every year on single-use capsules.

We offer you capsules that are perfectly compatible with your coffee machine so that you can use them every day with your favourite, freshly grinded coffee. All the varieties of coffee you can imagine, with all their intensities and textures, in a single capsule.
